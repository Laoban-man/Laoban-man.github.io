Brand anonymization (WIP)

Filter out brands in video and images

Branding is ubiquitous, it is on clothing, on cars, on television, on bus stops, and so forth. It can be overwhelming, companies fighting for your attention in a limited time or place. This - ongoing - project is an attempt to filter out invasive advertising from visual input.

With no ready-made dataset available for this project, we repurpose existing datasets and use annotating tools to prepare a dataset identifying instances of advertising.

  • Computer vision
